The Story of This Piece

Each casting begins with a gentle, non-toxic plaster formula developed specifically for use with newborn skin. We pour by hand, allow it to cure naturally, then sand, seal, and finish the surface in your chosen colour. The result is a museum-quality impression that captures every line, every crease, every detail of a moment that passed too quickly.
